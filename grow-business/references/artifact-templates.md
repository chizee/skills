# Artifact templates — grow-business

Skeletons this metaskill creates in the user's `docs/` folder. Extend-only artifacts (OFFER.md, STRATEGY.md, METRICS.md, CUSTOMER.md) are governed by the section headings named in each phase — read the file, add or update your sections, preserve everyone else's. Only the tracker and MARKETING.md (created by this journey) have full skeletons below.
